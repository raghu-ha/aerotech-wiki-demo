# AeroTech Industries — Titan 900 Engine Technical Specifications

**Document ID:** AT-ENG-T900-SPEC-001  
**Revision:** Rev 3.1  
**Date:** January 2026  
**Classification:** Technical Reference

---

## 1. Engine Overview

The AeroTech Titan 900 is a high-bypass turbofan engine designed for the AeroMax A80 wide-body aircraft. Certified for ETOPS-180 operations with a design service life of 25,000 flight cycles.

---

## 2. Key Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Engine Type | High-bypass turbofan |
| Thrust Rating | 68,000 – 82,000 lbf |
| Bypass Ratio | 8.5:1 |
| Overall Pressure Ratio | 38:1 |
| Fan Diameter | 112 inches (2.84m) |
| Fan Blade Count | 22 |
| Fan Blade Material | Carbon fibre composite with titanium leading edge |
| Engine Dry Weight | 6,100 kg |
| Max Operating Altitude | 43,000 ft |

## 5. Known Issues and Service Bulletins

### 5.1 Fan Blade Leading Edge Erosion
**Issue:** Accelerated erosion on fan blade leading edges in high-particulate environments.
**Impact:** Reduced efficiency, increased fuel burn
**Action:** Refer to Service Bulletin **SB-2024-047**
**Affected Parts:** AT-T900-FB-001 through AT-T900-FB-022

### 5.2 IP Compressor Tip Rub
**Issue:** Minor tip rub in Stage 3 after 800+ cycles in high-humidity operations.
**Impact:** EGT increase of 5-8°C above baseline
**Action:** Borescope inspection at next scheduled check
