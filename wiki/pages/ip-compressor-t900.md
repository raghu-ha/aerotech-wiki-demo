# IP Compressor (Titan 900)

## Summary
The 8-stage Intermediate Pressure Compressor of the Titan 900 engine. Subject to minor tip rub in Stage 3 after 800+ cycles in high-humidity operations.

## Technical Details
- Stages: 8
- Rotor Part Number: AT-T900-IPC-R001
- Inspection Interval: Every 1,000 flight cycles
- Known Issue: Stage 3 tip rub after 800+ cycles in high-humidity environments
  - Impact: EGT increase of 5–8°C above baseline

## Current Status
Active. Monitor for tip rub via borescope at next scheduled check if operating in high-humidity environments beyond 800 cycles.
