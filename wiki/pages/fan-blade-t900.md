# Fan Blade Rev A (AT-T900-FB-001) — SUPERSEDED

## Summary
Original carbon fibre composite fan blade for the Titan 900, superseded by Rev B (AT-T900-FB-002) following SB-2024-047. Must not be installed post-SB-2024-047. All remaining stock must be quarantined.

## Technical Details
- Part Numbers: AT-T900-FB-001 (blade), AT-T900-FB-LE-001 (leading edge strip)
- Material: Carbon fibre composite with titanium leading edge
- Original Inspection Interval: Every 500 flight cycles
- Replacement Threshold: Damage exceeding 5mm chord loss (5mm per original spec; 2mm per SB-2024-047 enhanced criteria)
- Known Issue: Accelerated leading edge erosion in high-particulate environments

### Supersession
| Part Number | Superseded By | Status |

## Current Status
**SUPERSEDED.** Must not be installed following SB-2024-047. All remaining stock must be quarantined per AT-ENG-T900-IPC-001 Rev 4.0.
