# Fan Blade Rev B (AT-T900-FB-002)

## Summary
Improved specification fan blade introduced by SB-2024-047 to replace superseded AT-T900-FB-001 blades. Rev B features improved leading edge bond and extends the inspection interval from 500 to 750 cycles. The current and only approved production fan blade for the Titan 900.

## Technical Details
- Part Number: AT-T900-FB-002 (Rev B)
- Replaces: AT-T900-FB-001 (SUPERSEDED — quarantine all stock)
- New Inspection Interval: 750 flight cycles (vs 500 for Rev A)
- Installed per SB-2024-047 compliance
- Qty per engine: 22
