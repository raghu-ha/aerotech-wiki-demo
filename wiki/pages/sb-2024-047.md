# Service Bulletin SB-2024-047

## Summary
MANDATORY service bulletin requiring inspection and replacement of Titan 900 fan blades (AT-T900-FB-001 through AT-T900-FB-022) due to accelerated leading edge erosion in high-particulate environments. Compliance required within 300 flight cycles. Replacement with Rev B blades (AT-T900-FB-002) extends inspection interval from 500 to 750 cycles.

## Technical Details

**Priority:** MANDATORY — within 300 flight cycles  
**Issued:** March 2024 (Rev 2.0)  
**Applies to:** Titan 900 Series Engines

### Failure Progression
| Stage | Cycles | Condition | Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Stage 1 | 0–300 | Minor pitting on leading edge | No performance impact |
| Stage 2 | 300–450 | Erosion >3mm chord loss | Fuel burn +0.3–0.8%; EGT margin –5–12°C |
| Stage 3 | 450+ | Erosion >5mm | MANDATORY REPLACEMENT; risk of blade fragment separation; Zone 1 vibration increase |

### Fleet Finding (45 engines inspected)
- 22% showed erosion exceeding 3mm before 500-cycle interval
- 5% exceeded 5mm threshold before 400 cycles
- One engine: asymmetric erosion causing vibration increase (Zone 1: 4.1 IPS vs limit 5.0 IPS)

### Required Actions

**Immediate (within 50 flight cycles):** Enhanced visual inspection per AT-AMM-T900-72-11-01.  
Rejection criteria:
- Chord loss exceeding 2mm
- Delamination at titanium-composite bond
- Any asymmetric erosion pattern

**Compliance (within 300 flight cycles):** Replace all fan blades with Rev B specification.
- Replacement Part: AT-T900-FB-002 (Rev B)
- New Inspection Interval: 750 cycles (was 500)
- Labour: 46 man-hours per engine

**Post-Replacement Checks:**
1. Ground engine run per AT-AMM-T900-71-00-01
2. Zone 1 vibration — must be below 3.5 IPS
3. EGT margin at takeoff — minimum 45°C required
4. ACMS baseline reset
