# Fan Assembly (FAN-T900)

## Summary
The Fan Assembly for the Titan 900 engine consists of 22 Rev B carbon fibre composite fan blades with titanium leading edges, mounted on a single fan disc. Assembly part number AT-T900-FAN-ASSY-001. It is the primary propulsive component of the high-bypass turbofan.

## Technical Details
- Assembly Part Number: AT-T900-FAN-ASSY-001
- Weight: 465 kg (complete assembly)
- Replacement Time: 70 man-hours
- Special Tools Required: AT-T900-BRT-001 (blade removal), AT-T900-FDT-001 (disc transport)
- Fan Diameter: 112 inches (2.84m)
- Blade Count: 22
- Blade Material: Carbon fibre composite with titanium leading edge
